About Paul Rothery

    I've been developing software for almost 30 years using various technologies. Most of the development has been back end server and database work. For the past several years I've been working on HP NonStop (Tandem) and Linux using Java with Spring and Hibernate. The databases I've been using are a variety of HP SQL/MX, Oracle, Sybase and MySQL.

    For the past few years I've been playing with a few things I don't get to do on a daily basis and will be adding and contributing to a few projects in this group.

    Other than development I like to do a lot of running trails, or roads to get to trails! I try to spend a lot of time in the mountains hiking and fishing.
