About

The Active Bean Coders is an open source coding group dedicated to writing better code. We have no memberships or fees. We simply get together and help each other write code. It's as simple as that.

We have all levels of experience. Some of our members have 20+ years of experience and are very talented at what they do. We have some who are just learning. Whever you are at, there is a spot for you.

Our philosophy

Our belief is based on a simple fundamental. If you want to become great at something, you need to learn it and then you need to do it. Then do it some more and learn from your mistakes. Repeat this process over and over until you master it. We believe this path to mastery is achieved easier with a team, rather than alone.

As we write code, we work together. Many of us write together, while others write and share their code seeking feedback and guidance. Most of our projects are smaller and target a specific technology. The goal is to write as many projects as possible while learning new skills.

The better we become as coders, the better job opportunities we have. As we perfect our skills, we are faced with more interesting problems to solve. If we have that perfect job, we have better job satisfaction. Our effort to become great at what we love to do is quite simple.

Our Values

We are a passionate group with a strong passion to learn and share our knowledge.

Our belief is you will get back more than what you put into the group. We call it 'Coding Karma'. Those with knowledge share and mentor those without. We mentor and sometimes 'push' each other to achieve their goals.

The 'Active' in our name is because we are actively involved in writing code and working together. We usually meet weekly. The 'Bean' in our name has a double meaning. We typically meet at a coffee shop and most of us write Java code.

Our place

We meet weekly on Sunday's at 6pm. Our meeting place is at Backstage Coffee in beautiful downtown Denver.
