Welcome to the Active Bean Coders. We are a passionate group of coders who formed in March 2014. Our home is beautiful Denver Colorado. We are an informal group open to all skills and abilities. The one requirement for our group is to have a passion to learn and share. We meet weekly on Sundays and work together on fun and interesting projects.

These pages are our sandbox. As a sandbox, our pages are heavily commented and are not perfect. The coder may be targeting a particular new technology or learning a new skillset. We will be consistently updating our web pages and project pages, so make sure to check our pages often.

We are always interested in having new members. All levels and abilities are welcome! Stop by and have a cup of coffee with us.
